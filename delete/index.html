How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to the account you wish to delete using the app.
  2. Go to your user page by tapping the profile icon at the bottom right corner.
  3. From the menu icon in the top right corner of your user page, select Settings.
  4. In the User section, select Account Settings.
  5. Choose Delete Account.
  6. Enter your account password and select Delete Account to confirm.
