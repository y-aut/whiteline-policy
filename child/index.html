Child Safety Standards Policy

Effective Date: 2025-02-03

1. Introduction

Our app prioritizes the safety of children and has established strict standards to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). To ensure a secure environment for all users, the following guidelines must be adhered to.

2. Prohibited Activities

  • Posting, sharing, or storing content that includes or promotes child sexual exploitation or abuse.
  • Distributing text, images, videos, or audio that depict children as sexual subjects.
  • Sending messages intended to engage in inappropriate contact, solicitation, or exploitation of children.
  • Publishing content that implies or suggests sexual acts involving children.
  • Collecting, sharing, or selling children's personal information without proper consent.

3. Preventive Measures

  • User Reporting Feature: A simple reporting function allows users to flag problematic content.
  • App-Level Age Restriction: The app itself is subject to age restrictions as set by Google Play to prevent access by underage users.
  • Awareness of Terms and Guidelines: All users are informed of our policies and must comply with them.
